Community Fridge – A Smart Food Sharing Platform

Overview

  • A sustainable, app-connected community fridge system that allows users to donate and take ingredients easily, reducing food waste and promoting local sharing.

Key Features

App Integration

  • Users can view available ingredients in the community fridge and explore recipe suggestions based on what’s inside.

Personal Fridge Monitoring

  • Users can track the freshness and expiry dates of their own ingredients (manually or via camera integration). When items are nearing expiration, the app will recommend donating them to the community fridge.

Gamified Ranking System

  • Users earn points and rankings based on their donation and usage activity, encouraging active participation and reducing waste.

Emergency support network app

Key features:

  • Emergency alert system
  • if people suddenly feel unwell or in danger, they can send their real-time location to nearby app users and their registered emergency contacts through app

Third-party rescue notification

  • if someone collapses or becomes unconscious, and cannot ask for help by themselves, people around them can ask for help to nearby app users through app

Messaging to paramedic and medical professionals

  • the user can message nearby professionals to tell situations for quick understanding
  • If no professionals are nearby, the user can message medical personnel for advice

AED locator

  • User can check the location of nearby AEDs on a map
  • when AED is needed, the app makes a automated call to the facility where it is located and notifies app users around it to request someone to bring it

Personal medical profile

  • each user can register their chronic illness, prescribed medications, allergies, primary physician information, etc.. for a safe and timely treatment
