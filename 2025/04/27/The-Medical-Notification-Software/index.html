The Medical Notification Software

2025-04-27

Concept: Software that allows medical workers, nearby people and hospitals to be notified in case of an emergency event. The aim of this software is to increase the chance of survival in case of an emergency that requires immediate medical treatment, due to the rise of response time for ambulances.

Application Concept

  • Term and Conditions

  • Location, Data Usage

  • Registration

  • Request access to fit activity, location service
    Gyroscope, accelerometer

  • Notify registered medical workers and hospital within 1km of radius

  • Check nearby AED station and notify people around it to bring
    AED location on map

  • Messaging to paramedic and medical professionals

  • Personal medical profile
